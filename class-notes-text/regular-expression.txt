. - period/wildcard
+ - one or more
* - zero or more
? - optional/zero or one
() - grouping
[] - range replacement for 
{minmax} - minimum and maximum value same
{min,max} - min and max seperate value
| - OR operator
^ - begining
$ - end of the line
\ - escape character
\d - digits
\w - characters
\s - space
